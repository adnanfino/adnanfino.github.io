Who Am I?

My name is Adnan Fino, I am 18 years old and I was born in Damascus, Syria 2000. In Turkey I graduated from year 11, then we moved to Manchester in the UK. In Manchester I studied ESOL course to improve my language for six months till I got my level 1 English Functional skills certificate. In 2017, I applied to level 1 IT course in The Manchester College. After one month in that course, my tutor gave me the opportunity to study level 2 Computer Science instead of studying level 1 Digital Technology. I liked this course and I did well in all the units, in the same time I improved my IT skills because I learned many things that related to websites development, Graphics design, computer networks, software development and other units during the course. My next step will be level 3 courses, because my aim is to continue my study till the university. In future I will study Biomedical Engineering, and I will make this world a better place to live.
